Creat radiant skin

  1. Wash your face. Wash your face to remove any unwanted oils or old makeup.
  2. Moisturize your skin. You should always moisturize after washing your face.
  3. Spread primer onto your skin. Primer is a makeup product that fills your pores and smooths your skin in preparation for foundation.
  4. Apply a layer of foundation. Foundation evens out your skin tone and gives you a beautiful glow.
